# Sports-Psychology-Data-Profiling
This project addresses the assessment and intervention of an athlete using tools such as the IPED (strengths profile), the ACANCODO questionnaire (communication), and attention tests like the D2 and grids. It includes self concept assessments (AF5), stress management assessments (CAN/CAP), and mental training proposals using NLP and self-monitoring

This repository showcases the comprehensive practical project **"Training a Sports Psychologist"**. It documents a professional-grade psychological evaluation and intervention process designed to optimize athletic performance through psychometric data and evidence-based mental training.

## Project Overview

The project follows a structured clinical-sporting methodology, transitioning from initial diagnostic assessments to the design of specific psychological intervention plans.

### 1. Communication Channels Analysis
* **Tool:** ACANCODO Questionnaire.
* **Objective:** Identifying dominant sensory channels (Visual, Auditory, Kinesthetic) to enhance the feedback loop between the athlete and the coaching staff.

### 2. Psychological Performance Inventory (IPED)
* Assessment of 7 critical variables: Self-confidence, Negative/Positive Coping Control, Attentional Control, Visuomotor Control, and Activation Level.
* Identification of competitive strengths and psychological blind spots.

### 3. Specialized Psychometric Testing
* **Self-Concept (AF5):** Analysis across Social, Academic/Professional, Emotional, Family, and Physical dimensions.
* **Attention & Concentration:** Evaluated via the **d2 Test** and **Concentration Grids** (Grid Test) to measure processing speed and cognitive fatigue resistance.
* **Coping Strategies (CAP/CAN):** Diagnostic of stress management mechanisms during high-pressure competition.

### 4. Mental Training Intervention Proposals
* **NLP Techniques:** Design of "Anchoring" sessions based on Neuro-Linguistic Programming.
* **Monitoring Protocols:** Implementation of self-registration logs for physiological (HR, sleep) and psychological (mood, perceived effort) variables.
* **Attentional Strengthening:** Specific drills designed to improve focus and concentration consistency.

## 🛠️ Tools & Methodology
* **MenPas Platform:** Online assessment tools for statistical data collection and psychometric validation.
* **Quantitative & Qualitative Analysis:** Integration of standardized test scores with behavioral observation.
* **Evidence-Based Practice:** Following official scales and sports psychology literature (Brickenkamp, Hernández-Mendo, et al.).
